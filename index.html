Aggie Identity Verification

Help us protect Aggieland from cyber threats by verifying your identity.

Get Started

This service uses Persona, a third-party verification service. Data collected using this service is subject to Persona's privacy policy and the Texas A&M Division of Information Technology's identity verification data handling policy.

Persona's process uses your government-issued picture identification and a live "selfie" to verify your identity. After your verification is finished, Help Desk Central will receive a notification of whether the process was successful.

  • If the process is successful, no further action on your part is required and Help Desk Central will assist you in resetting your password.
  • If the verification is not successful, Help Desk Central personnel will assist you with an alternate identity verification process.

Note: Please make sure you have your driver's license (or alternate government-issued ID) ready.
