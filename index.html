Get Started

Note: Please make sure you have your driver's license (or alternate government-issued ID) ready.

This service uses Persona. Data collected using this service is subject to Persona's privacy policy and the Division of IT's identity verification data handling policy.

Persona compares your government-issued photo ID and a live "selfie" to verify your identity. After your verification is finished, Help Desk Central will receive a notification.

  • If verification is successful, Help Desk Central will assist you in resetting your password.
  • If verification is not successful, Help Desk Central will assist you in an alternate identity verification process.
